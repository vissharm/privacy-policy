Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Important Notice:

Our Educational Guidance App is designed with your privacy as the top priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All app functionality works locally on your device without any data collection or server communication.

How Our App Works

  • All content is stored locally within the app
  • Your selections (class, stream, etc.) are processed entirely on your device
  • No information is sent to any external servers
  • No user tracking or analytics are implemented
  • No user accounts or registration required

Data Storage

The app uses local device storage only for:

  • Storing your preferences temporarily during app usage
  • Caching educational content for faster access
  • All data is cleared when you uninstall the app

App Permissions

Our app requires minimal permissions:

  • Internet access: Only to display external educational resources when explicitly requested
  • No access to contacts, location, or other device features

Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for children as:

  • We collect no personal information from anyone, including children
  • All content is educational and pre-loaded
  • No interactive features that could compromise privacy

Third-Party Services

Our app:

  • Does not integrate with any third-party services
  • Does not use analytics tools
  • Does not contain advertisements

Updates to Privacy Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will post the updated version in the app and on this page.

Consent
